# Product

Raqm is a private tax preparation workspace that helps Pakistanis organize, calculate, and prepare their tax data before filing on FBR Iris.

The MVP supports salaried filers, local encrypted storage, salary and withholding input, bank profit, basic assets/liabilities, a rules-based estimate, Iris-ready summary, checklist, and exports.

Raqm must not be positioned as an AI accountant, FBR replacement, guaranteed legal filing system, or accountant replacement for complex cases.
